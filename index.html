This collection of slides form the course materials for the Webscripting2 — Serverside Webscripting course, part of the Professional Bachelor ICT study programme, taught at KaHo Sint-Lieven, Ghent, Belgium.

The materials were developed by Bram(us) Van Damme, lecturer ICT at KaHo Sint-Lieven, who blogs over at bram.us and Twitters as @bramus.

The materials may be used freely, as long as credit to Bramus is present and the top right graphical link to ikdoeict.be remains in place.

Suggestions and additions may be mailed to Bramus, or sent via a pull request on GitHub.
